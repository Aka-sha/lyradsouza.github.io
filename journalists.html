Out-of-work journalists looking to find meaningful work are invited to apply for a mentorship position through JournalistxEdu!

Mentors will work with up to ten students over a semester-long period and will recieve a stipend as compensation for sharing their expertise. A list of benchmarks that students are expected to reach will be provided to mentors as a guide for them in creating a mini-curriculum.

Mentors are encouraged to structure their lessons around the disciplines they are most passionate about and have the most experience with.

The time commitment for mentors will be comprised of weekly meetings with students, lesson plans, and writing thoughtful feedback.

Feel free to reach out to us with any questions or comments at journalistxedu@gmail.com or follow us on Instagram at @journalistxedu!
