Inspiring the students of today to write the stories of tomorrow.

JournalistxEdu is a mentorship program designed to teach students practical writing skills and introduce them to the field of journalism. We pair out-of-work professionals with high school students in a four week program that involves writing assignments, meaningful feedback, and a chance for students to publish their own work.
