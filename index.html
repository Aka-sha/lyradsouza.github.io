Where did all the teachers go?

There is a growing teacher shortage in k-12 schools across the nation, but in Window Rock Unified School District 8, the need is more pressing than ever.

With a staggering 44.4% of children living in poverty and only 11.3% of the population in possession of a college diploma, the odds are not stacked in the children's favor.
