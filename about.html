JournalistxEdu is a mentorship program that connects out-of-work journalists with high school students to strengthen students’ writing skills and increase exposure to the field of local journalism.

Journalists and students alike are invited to apply to the program through our website. The application form is brief and serves as a way for us to learn about our customers and their fields of interest.

Accepted students will be matched with a professional journalist in a semester (roughly four months) long mentorship program that focuses on guiding the participants through writing assignments, discussion, and feedback from peers and professionals. Students will have the chance to publish their writing on our website.

Feel free to reach out to us with any questions or comments at journalistxedu@gmail.com or follow us on Instagram at @journalistxedu!
